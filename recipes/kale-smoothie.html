Dairy Free Green Smoothie (Vegan Kale Smoothie)

Description

This dairy free green smoothie is the perfect way to kick off your morning. Loaded with a delicious, healthy combination of foods, you won’t be able to wait for another one of these vegan green smoothies.

Ingredients

  • 1 ½ cup coconut water
  • 1 medium orange (can also use ¼ cup orange juice or 2-3 clementines)
  • 1 cup kale, packed
  • ½ cup rolled oats
  • 1 tablespoon chia seeds
  • 1 teaspoon agave
  • 1 frozen banana (or fresh- see notes)

Steps

  1. In a blender, add all the ingredients.
  2. Blend until well combined.
  3. *If using a fresh banana, add ice if desired
